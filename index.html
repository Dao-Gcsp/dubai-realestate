UAE Golden Visa

Invest. Reside. Belong.

The UAE Golden Visa grants long-term residency to property investors, offering security, freedom, and access to one of the world's most progressive economies.

⚑

10-Year Renewable Residency

Live, work, and travel freely with long-term visa security for you and your family.

◎

Family Sponsorship

Sponsor your spouse, children, and parents under a single property investment.

◈

No Minimum Stay Required

Maintain your visa regardless of how many days per year you spend in the UAE.

◇

Business & Banking Freedom

Open UAE bank accounts, establish businesses, and access DIFC financial services.
